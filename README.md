# The-Spider-Crab-and-MAST-CAM
A rover (rough "perserverance-replica") equipped with rocker bogie (with a differential bar) suspension, with a now updated RASSOR-less mechanism, and a camera module.  Modeled around the STS-3215 servo.

Intrustion to print:
All files are named BuildPlate_ASA#1-8, they are to be printed in ASA with an minimum infill of 60% with the sole exception of BuildPlate_ASA#5 which is the chassis and can be printed with an minimum infill of 40%. Note the both BuildPlate_ASA#1 and BuildPlate_ASA#2 are the exact same thing, I just found it easier to put in that way instead of telling people to print BuildPlate_ASA#1 twice. 

BuildPlate_TPU#1-2 are the wheels and are meant to be printed with TPU at an infill of 30%, even though they may look similar THEY ARE NOT THE SAME. Do not copy the wheels and print them.
